Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-30

Schreiben auf Deutsch ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. The App is designed to function with minimal personal data collection:

  • User Inputs: When you generate a writing task or request feedback, the text you provide is sent to third-party AI providers (Google Gemini and Groq) for processing.
  • Local Data & Backups: Your writing history, favorite tasks, and progress are stored locally on your device using an encrypted database. If enabled in your device settings, this data may be backed up to your personal Google Drive via the Android Auto-Backup service. We do not have access to this data on our servers.
  • Device Information: We do not collect unique device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID) or personal contact information.

2. How Your Information is Used

The information processed by the App is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To generate customized German writing exercises.
  • To provide AI-driven feedback and corrections on your writing.
  • To save your progress and preferences locally or in your personal cloud backup.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide AI features, the App uses the following third-party services. These services have their own privacy policies regarding how they handle data sent via API:

  • Google Gemini (Google AI Studio): Used for content generation and analysis.
  • Groq (Llama Cloud): Used as a fallback provider for AI analysis.
  • Pollinations.ai: Used to generate decorative images for writing tasks based on text prompts.

Please note that while your text inputs are sent to these providers for processing, we do not send any personally identifiable information (PII) to them.

4. Data Retention

All your practice data remains on your device. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data (tasks, progress, favorites) will be permanently deleted from your device.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
